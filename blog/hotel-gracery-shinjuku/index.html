Hotel Gracery Shinjuku

The Hotel Gracery is located in Kabukichō, Tokyo, just northeast of Shinjuku station. It looks something like an Alienware tower, especially when viewed from the street as pictured above. Notably, the cinema attached to the hotel features a full-size Godzilla head which can be seen peeking over the rooftop.

The hotel charges extra for ‘Godzilla-view’ rooms.
