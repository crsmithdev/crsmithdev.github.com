Building Better Email Habits with Mailbox

March 26, 2013

9 minute read

Building Better Email Habits with Mailbox Introduction As my first-ever blog post, I’m presenting my recent experience with the recent email app, Mailbox. This is less about the app itself, however (or it recently being snapped up by Dropbox for $100 million), but more about how I used it to help me examine and improve my behavior when it comes to handling and communicating via email. Mailbox Briefly, Mailbox is an iPhone app that integrates aggressively with one or more of your email accounts (GMail only, for now), and through a gesture-focused UI presents four actions to be taken on any one email: archive it, delete it, reschedule it for later, or send it to a list.
